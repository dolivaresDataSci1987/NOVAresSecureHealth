# Dashboard Specification — NOVAres SecureHealth

## General objective

Provide an intuitive business-facing dashboard to explore the insurance portfolio, understand risk, detect anomalies, review pricing adequacy, and recommend plans for new prospects.

## Pages

### Home
Executive summary of the project and main KPIs.

### Portfolio Overview
- number of insured members
- active policies
- premium totals
- claim totals
- portfolio composition by plan, region, age, archetype

### Risk Scoring
- member risk score distribution
- risk segments
- main drivers
- individual member lookup

### Fraud & Abuse
- suspicious members
- suspicious providers
- alert reasons
- severity filters

### Pricing Intelligence
- premium vs expected cost
- underpriced / overpriced groups
- premium guidance simulation

### Prospect Profiler
- survey-based profile
- archetype prediction
- risk estimate
- plan recommendation
